What is QRToolkit?

QRToolkit is a free, browser-based tool that lets you create and decode QR codes instantly — no app installs, no sign-up required. Everything runs locally in your browser for fast, private use.

Example Uses
  • Share website links, convert any URL into a scannable code for posters, cards, or screens
  • WiFi credentials, let guests connect to your network instantly by scanning
  • Contact info & business cards, encode your name, phone, and email as a digital vCard
  • Event tickets & check-ins, generate unique codes for attendee verification
  • Product information, link a QR to product pages, manuals, or videos
  • Social media & documents, share profiles, Google Docs, or Drive folders with a single scan

How to Use

Generating a QR Code
  1. 1Go to the Generator tab
  2. 2Enter your text, URL, or any data
  3. 3Customize color, background, and size
  4. 4Add an optional description (appears in download)
  5. 5Click Generate QR Code
  6. 6Download as PNG or JPG
Scanning / Decoding
  1. 1Go to the Scanner tab
  2. 2Upload or drag-and-drop a QR code image
  3. 3Click Decode QR Code
  4. 4The decoded data appears in the result box
  5. 5Non-QR images show an error message
